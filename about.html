About Us

Japanese Restaurant

Our Story

Walking through the forest in North Kyoto, the air is crisp, the wind is gentle and the clean serene calms the soul. Morico captures this peaceful feeling and transforms our stores by instilling this sense of confidence & vibrance.
The journey has been long.The roads exhaustive & our feet are weary… But along this road, we have created food journals, collected food memmories and compiled a wealth of experience culmulating in the creation of Morico.
From a quite corner with a cup of authentic Japanese Matcha for contemplation, to a small table of friends for a joyous desset treat, onto a festive family dinner. Morico was designed to be your café, your restaurant, your dining room.
